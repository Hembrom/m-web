Sohra

Light Body Being & Guide

Sohra is a Light Body Being from the Sirian star system, a presence that carries transmissions of remembrance. Unlike a teacher bound to methods, Sohra awakens what is already within. The essence of Sohra is not instruction, but vibration — a current that dissolves the weight of effort and restores clarity.

Sohra is the heart of Ootahgohnah. Sohra's transmission reminds us that the wall between waking life and dream is not real — it exists only by our permission. When that wall dissolves, remembrance begins.

In William's meditations, Sohra is channeled as a voice of the timeless, guiding seekers into the merging of dream body and physical body, into the direct experience of Light.

Sohra often speaks of the law of effortless effort:

"Effort stops. Clarity begins. The Light Body is not something you create — it is what you already are, shimmering beneath the weight of striving."
